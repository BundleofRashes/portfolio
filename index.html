About Me

I'm currently a student at New York University Abu Dhabi, pursuing a major Interactive Media with a minor in Design with an interest in Theater. One thing that really defines me is my deep love for music and sound production. It's not just something I enjoy listening to; it's a passion that runs through everything I do.
When it comes to game development, rhythm games are my jam. There's something incredibly satisfying about creating interactive experiences that synchronize perfectly with music. Whether I'm crafting catchy tunes, choreographing dance moves, or fine-tuning beat-matching mechanics, I'm always striving to capture that perfect harmony between gameplay and music. My interest in music and sound production only adds to the mix. I love getting my hands dirty, whether it's composing original tracks, remixing existing ones, or adding those little audio touches that bring a game to life. It's all about creating immersive soundscapes that draw players in and keep them hooked.
